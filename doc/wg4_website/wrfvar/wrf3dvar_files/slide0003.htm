Decisions about first version of 3DVAR (continued)
Treatment of errors
•Observation error covariance matrix diagonal (use best estimate of error variances)
•Background errors homogeneous and isotropic, obtained via educated guess or “NMC” method (eventually definition of B should depend upon obs)
•Application of background error covariances via recursive filtering along the three coordinate directions; anisotropic  covariances will appear in research version
